# Geometry — ProofCoach Knowledge Base

## Triangle Fundamentals

**Area formulas**:
- $A = \frac{1}{2} bh$
- $A = \frac{abc}{4R}$ (circumradius $R$)
- $A = rs$ (inradius $r$, semiperimeter $s$)
- $A = \frac{1}{2}ab\sin C$

**Similar triangles**: AA, SAS, SSS similarity criteria.

---

## Circle Theorems

**Inscribed angle**: An inscribed angle is half the central angle subtending the same arc.

**Power of a point**: For a point $P$ and circle with center $O$, radius $r$:
- If $P$ is outside: $PA \cdot PB = PC \cdot PD$ (for any two chords through $P$)
- $\text{pow}(P) = d^2 - r^2$ where $d = OP$

**Ptolemy's theorem**: For a cyclic quadrilateral $ABCD$:
$$AC \cdot BD = AB \cdot CD + AD \cdot BC$$

---

## Law of Sines and Cosines

$$\frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C} = 2R$$

$$c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab\cos C$$

---

## Ceva's and Menelaus' Theorems

**Ceva's**: Cevians $AD$, $BE$, $CF$ are concurrent iff:
$$\frac{AF}{FB} \cdot \frac{BD}{DC} \cdot \frac{CE}{EA} = 1$$

**Menelaus'**: Points $D$, $E$, $F$ on sides (or extensions) of $\triangle ABC$ are collinear iff:
$$\frac{AF}{FB} \cdot \frac{BD}{DC} \cdot \frac{CE}{EA} = -1 \text{ (signed ratios)}$$

---

## Coordinate Geometry

**Distance**: $d = \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2 + (y_2-y_1)^2}$

**Area via shoelace**: $A = \frac{1}{2}|x_1(y_2-y_n) + x_2(y_3-y_1) + \cdots + x_n(y_1-y_{n-1})|$

**Circle**: $(x-h)^2 + (y-k)^2 = r^2$
